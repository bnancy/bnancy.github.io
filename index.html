My work

Newborn and children

Every parent has a story to tell when they talk about their children, their birth story, their milestones, and their special moments. Bee Nancy Creations focuses on creating unique items to tell just that, your child's unique personality and stories. Our passion is to make your job easy finding the best for your little ones.


Knitted and Handsewn

Bee Nancy Creations creates items that are handsewn, knitted, crochetted and leatherworks. The creations include maternity hospital robes, maternity photography props, newborn props, hats, socks and accessories; children's handmade cotumes, knitted items and handmade shoes.


Arts and Crafts

Bee Nancy Creations passion is to create special items that you would not resist to take home, these creations include homemade door decorations, indoor and ourdoor decorations, special holiday decorations, photo props and more. Our special items and decorations for the holidays are always the highlight on our homepage.

Special Occasions

Bee Nancy Creations also focuses on making those special milestones and special occasions unique, fresh and fun. These creations include birthday banners, birthday outfits and hats, first day of school props, and children unique items to match their unique style and personality.
